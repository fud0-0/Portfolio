Fudo Optic

I picked the name Fudo from the anime Devilman Crybaby, a Japanese word that refers to a Buddhist deity who symbolizes resilience. Optic refers to the visuals. Post Covid, around the age of 15, I started this as a passion project with no particular aim but to showcase stories of well-deserved places, thoughts and people. They are conveyed visually, verbally and acoustically.

Photographing Tools: Olympus EP 5, iPhone 5, Galaxy A35

Editing Tools: Snapseed, VSCO, Kuji Film, Samsung Gallery, Apple Photos, Phonto, Dafont.com
